I'd had my eye on a stay at Millie the Hut for a while, and our winter weekend here certainly lived up to expectations. We've stayed in a few shepherd's huts before, but Millie was by far the most comfortable and well equipped. The outside space was gorgeous - totally useable even in winter - and the sparkling clear brook was glorious. We're already thinking about when we can return, and fall asleep to the sound of the water burbling and the tawny owls hooting. Thank you Paul and Glenys for being so welcoming, friendly and helpful!
