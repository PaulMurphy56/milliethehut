This was the first time we had stayed in a place like this and we weren't sure whether it would be our cup of tea. How wrong we were. Paul was brilliant pre-visit, sending us several emails about our stay. His directions were excellent and both he and his wife were there to meet us and settle us in to the hut. Perfect hosts. We were delighted with its location, sitting as it does in its own field next to a beautiful stream. The Hut is well proportioned and well equipped with everything you need. We really appreciated the welcome pack (especially the fantastic orange-yolk eggs and local cider!) Adjacent to the hut is a lovely 'Shack' with fire pit etc. It's really quiet, just the sound of running water and bird song and as evening comes on the birds are replaced by numerous bats skimming over the stream. We were really pleased with Presteigne which is a short walk away. It has several pubs, restaurants and shops, everything we needed. The countryside round about is also ridiculously lovely and we did a great walk into the hills, with fantastic views over Wales and Herefordshire. We'll certainly come again.
