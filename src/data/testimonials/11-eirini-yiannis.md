We had a lovely weekend at Millie the Hut. Paul and Glenys were wonderful hosts. They were very helpful with suggestions of activities around the area and the welcome folder details were all very much appreciated. The location itself is exquisite, as it's very quiet and with wonderful natural views all around. The firepit and blankets provided were a lovely touch as we spent both nights outside enjoying the fire and stargazing. Ten out of ten.
