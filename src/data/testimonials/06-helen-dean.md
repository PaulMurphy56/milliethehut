We had a wonderful long weekend at Millie the Hut. It is in a beautiful, quiet spot. We loved cooking in the outside area on the BBQ and also enjoyed a fire whilst sat by the brook at night. We did some lovely local walks, so no need to use the car once you arrive. There was also a lovely welcome from our hosts and a very generous welcome pack. We could not fault our stay and hope to return.
