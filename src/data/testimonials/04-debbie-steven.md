Magical stay at Millie's. Couldn't fault it - Paul and Glenys really have thought of everything. From the fresh meadow flowers in a jug to the beautiful scented bathroom products to the selection of 6 different teabags in the kitchen - just delightful. Not forgetting the generous hamper - all gluten free to accommodate my coeliac husband! 10/10 for attention to detail. It never feels cramped with 'The Shack' adjacent - the most romantic restaurant table for 2 ever - and the firepit by the river, doubling as an amazing spot to meditate at dawn! Lovely, friendly owners too. Ten out of ten!
